PetVerse Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how PetVerse ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application ("the App").

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email Address: Required for account creation and authentication
  • User ID: Automatically generated for account management

Content Information

  • Pet Photos: Images you upload are processed by our AI transformation service
  • Transformed Content: AI-generated images and audio created from your photos

Usage Information

  • App Analytics: How you interact with the app to improve our services
  • Crash Reports: Technical data to fix bugs and improve performance

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI pet transformation services
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To store and retrieve your created content
  • To improve our app features and user experience
  • To provide customer support

Information Sharing and Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide our app functionality:

  • Firebase (Google): User authentication, data storage, and analytics
  • OpenAI: AI image transformation processing
  • Eleven Labs: AI voice generation
  • Stripe: Payment processing for subscriptions

These services may have access to your information as necessary to perform their functions, but are not permitted to use your information for other purposes.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may delete your account and associated data at any time through the app settings.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of certain data collection practices

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
