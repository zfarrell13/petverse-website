Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a pet transformation?
  1. Open the PetVerse app
  2. Tap the camera icon or "Upload Photo" on the Home tab
  3. Select a clear photo of your pet from your camera roll or take a new photo
  4. Enter your pet's name in the text field
  5. Tap "Generate" and wait for the AI to create your transformation
  6. Enjoy your unique pet character with voice-over!
Why do I need to create an account?
Creating an account allows you to:
  • Save your transformations to the Gallery
  • Access your creations across devices
  • Manage your subscription
  • Keep your pet art safe and accessible
What subscription plans are available?

Free: Create an account and explore the app

Premium ($4.99/month):

  • Unlimited pet transformations
  • Advanced artistic styles
  • High-resolution exports
  • Priority processing
  • Extended voice options
  • Watermark-free downloads
How do I cancel my subscription?
  1. Open the PetVerse app
  2. Go to Settings tab
  3. Tap "Billing"
  4. Tap "Manage Subscription"
  5. Follow the prompts to cancel

You can also manage your subscription through your App Store account settings.

What types of pet photos work best?
For the best transformations:
  • Use clear, well-lit photos
  • Make sure your pet is the main subject
  • Avoid heavily filtered or edited images
  • Photos with your pet facing the camera work great
  • Both close-up and full-body shots can work
How long does it take to generate a transformation?

Most transformations complete within 30-60 seconds. Processing time may vary based on:

  • Image complexity and size
  • Current server load
  • Your internet connection speed

Premium subscribers get priority processing for faster results.

Can I share my pet transformations?

Absolutely! You can:

  • Share directly to social media apps
  • Send via messaging apps
  • Save to your device's photo library
  • Export high-resolution versions (Premium)
Is my data safe and private?

Yes! We take privacy seriously:

  • Your photos are processed securely
  • We don't share your personal information
  • You can delete your account and data anytime
  • Read our full Privacy Policy for details
The app isn't working properly. What should I do?

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Force close and restart the app
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Restart your device
  4. Update to the latest version of PetVerse
  5. Make sure you have iOS 15.0 or later

If problems persist, contact us with details about the issue.

System Requirements
